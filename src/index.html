Composition

This design system consists of two major parts: components and structures. Components are small units of design. When components are put together with other components they form structures.

Components and structures.

A webpage is simply a collection of structures pieced together. Think of it this way:

{Headings, text, form elements} > {Header} > {Webpage}

{Mirror, window, frame, electronics} > {Door} > {Car}

{Components} > {Structure} > {Webpage}
